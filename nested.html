City of Light: Lightbearing

Summary:

Lightbearing is a genetic ability in which an individual is able to absorb light from the various celestial bodies and use it to gain supernatural abilities Each celestial body grants a different ability; a notable trend is that a sun and moon will provide abilities that are linked in some way. The amount of light a user has stored is measured in what is known as a 'Lum'. Conversely, the drain a given ability has on volume of light stored is what's called 'Dispersion Rate'. Any given Lightbearer can store around 100 Lums before experiecing adverse effects, such as melting.

Furthermore, certain minerals can also store a charge of light, providing a means to store different types of light. The disperation rate, and volume of light a mineral is able to store is dependant on size and mineral type. A notable connection has been made between these minerals and a linking celestial body; there are ten in total.

It should be noted that Lightbearers have been unable to draw upon the power of Mysus or Axien.
