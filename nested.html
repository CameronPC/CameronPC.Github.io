Magic and Technology, the importance it holds on a world

Magic and the type of technology that goes into a world can be some of the most memorable portion of whatever world you look into. For example; when you think of Avatar the Last Airbender, the first thought in your mind is probably the bending, aka that worlds version of magic.

Diagram of the Monomyth

When you think of One Piece one of the most prominenet features of that world is the devil fruit, and haki that people have, which again are that worlds "magic". For worlds without magic or anything of the sort we usually have a certain technological age they may be in. For example, Cyberpunk 2077, a game without magic, but time period is so advanced that the technology is extremely futuristic, and it is one of the big ideas when you think of the game.
