"City of Light"

Setting:

A steampunk-style city set on the fulcrum of the world which stretches almost down to the core. The bottom layers don't experience much light at all. The city itself is massive in scale, more akin to an entire state than a city. It has been built upon itself recursively due to limited space. Stretching the bounds of physical space, Pale Barrens stunt the outward growth of the city. The Pale Barrens themselves are a wasteland, scorched frequently by the celestic lights, while just past that lies the edge of the world, where the sky touches ground. Nobody has survived to see this far, however.

Lore:

The City and the Pale Barrens

The City of Light sits directly center of the world, with the Pale Barrens at the extremes. Due to it's massive size, the climate of the City of Light can change drastically depending on location, both horizontal or vertical, however anywhere within the city is considered habitable by humans. The Pale Barrens existing outside of the city limits, while they hold many sought after resources, also are terribly caustic, capable of inflicting physical harm on anyone stupid enough to venture outwards. Under direct sun/moonlight, an individual will begin to go through a process akin to 'melting'. After approximately seven seconds of direct exposure, the individual will be effectively dead, and will turn into a Dreg in tandum with the current celestial cycle.

Celestial Bodies and Cycles

The City of Light experiences day and night differently. There are five varying suns and moons, which cycle through on a daily basis. Weeks are five days long due to this fact. Each celestial body has a unique appearance as well as special properties, both represented in their light (see: Magic), and an associated mineral. The celestial bodies are as follows:
